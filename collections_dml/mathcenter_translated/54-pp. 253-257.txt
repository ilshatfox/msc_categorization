-TYPE INEQUALITY FOR THE DERIVATIVES OF THE CONJUGATE RATIONAL FUNCTION ON A SEGMENT  T.S. Mardvilko, A.A. Pekarskii  In the present paper, the conjugate function is considered. A Szegö-type inequality for the derivatives of the conjugate rational function on a closed interval was obtained. This inequality was proved when we investigated the relationship between the rate of the best uniform rational approximations of a function and the rates of the best uniform piecewise polynomial approximations.
