ON NUMERICAL SOLVING FOR A CLASS OF INTEGRO-DIFFERENTIAL EQUATIONS  N.S. Gabbasov  We study a linear integro-differential equation with a coefficient that has zeros of finite order. For its approximate solution in the space of generalized functions, we suggest and justify a special generalized variant of the collocation method. Keywords: integro-differential equation, space of generalized functions, approximate solution, collocation method, theoretical substantiation.
