Pershagin  The article gives the justification of the polynomial Galerkin method for solving boundary value problems for ordinary integro–differential equations when the order of the internal differential operator is higher than the corresponding external one. The rate of convergence of the method is characterized in terms of the best polynomial approximations of the exact solution, which automatically responds to the smoothness properties of the coefficients of the equation. Keywords: Sobolev space, Jacobi–Gegenbauer weight, integro-differential equation, Cauchy problem, polynomial approximation, Galerkin method, convergence of the method.
