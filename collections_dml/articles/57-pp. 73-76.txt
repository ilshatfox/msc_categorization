FACTORIZATION OF THE CHARACTERISTIC POLYNOMIAL OF JACOBIAN MATRIX  A.V. Bratishchev  Let an autonomous system of n-th order differential equations have an invariant set. For the equilibrium state on this set, we form the Jacobian matrix for the right hand sides of the system. The paper proposes a formula for factorization of this matrix. It is useful in the problem of designing controllers of dynamic systems. Keywords: autonomous system, invariant set, the state of equilibrium, Jacobian matrix, characteristic polynomial.
