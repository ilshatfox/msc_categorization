By Pn we denote the set of all polinomials in C of degree at most n ∈N. In 1887 D.I. Mendeleev [8, § 86] posed the following problem: for a polynomial f ,  deg f = 2, with | f (x)| ≤ M for x ∈ [a,b], give an estimation of | f ′(x)| for x ∈ [a,b]. In the book of V.I. Smirnov and N.A.
