Novikov  We prove that there exists a matrix of interpolation nodesMγ with the properties: 1)Mγ is arbitrarily close to the matrix of equidistant nodes T = {xk,n = 2πk/(2n + 1) : −n ≤ k ≤ n, n = 1,2, . . . }; 2) any function f ∈C2π can be adjusted in a set of arbitrarily small measure such that the (0,2,3)-interpolation process of adjusted continuous function g based on the nodesMγ will be uniformly convergent to g on [−π,π]. Keywords: lacunary interpolation, Fourier series, adjustment of functions.
