Fedotov  We obtain an estimate of the norm of the Hermite-Fejer interpolation operator in one-dimensional and multidimensional Sobolev spaces. It is shown that in the one-dimensional case the norm of this operator is bounded. In the multidimensional case the norm of this operator depends on the choice of the nodes.
