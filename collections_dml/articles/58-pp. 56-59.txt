Fadeeva  The work is devoted to the further study of sums with fractional fractions of an exponential function. The estimation of the convergence rate in the multidimensional limit theorem with large deviations for sums of functions of independent random variables is improved. A consequence of this theorem is the limit theorem for the distribution of the modulus of a trigonometric sum with an exponential function.
