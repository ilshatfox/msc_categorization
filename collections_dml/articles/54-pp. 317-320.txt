NEW METHOD SOLUTION OF THE INHOMOGENEOUS RIEMANN BOUNDARY VALUE PROBLEM WITH INFINITE INDEX ON THE BEAM  R.B. Salimov, A.Z. Suleymanov  We consider the Riemann boundary value problem with infinite index when boundary condition of the problem is defined on the positive real axis of the complex plane. To solve this problem, we use an approach based on removal of the infinite gap of the argument of the coefficient in the boundary condition with a specially chosen analytic function. Keywords: Riemann boundary value problem, analytic function, infinite index.
