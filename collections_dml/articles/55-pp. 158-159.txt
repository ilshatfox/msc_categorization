HOMOGENEOUS HILBERT BOUNDARY VALUE PROBLEM WITH SEVERAL POINTS OF TURBULENCE  P.L. Shabalin, A.Kh. Fatykhov  In the upper half-plane we consider the Riemann-Hilbert boundary-value problem with infinite index. Its coefficient is Holder continuous everywhere, except of a finite set of points, where its argument has discontinuities of the second order. We investigate existence and uniqueness of solution and describe the set of solutions in the case of nonuniqueness. Our methodology is based on the theory of entire functions and the geometric theory of functions. Keywords: Riemann-Hilbert boundary value problem, existence, uniqueness of solution, entire functions, infinite index.
