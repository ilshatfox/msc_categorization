Razdobudko  An effective numerical scheme was developed to solve the equation of transfer of the concentration of the sorbed substance by a single-phase liquid in a porous medium, and the analysis of its convergence by the spatial step of the calculated grid was carried out. The design scheme uses local analytical integration of reversible sorption kinetics and allows a significant increase in time step.
