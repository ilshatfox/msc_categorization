KELDYSH PROBLEM FOR EQUATION OF MIXED TYPE WITH CHARACTERISTIC DEGENERATION IN RECTANGLE DOMAIN  Yu.K. Sabitova  We study the Keldysh problem for equation of mixed type with characteristic degeneration in a rectangular domain. A solution to the problem is constructed in the form of the sum of a series. We prove the theorems of uniqueness and existence of the solution of the problem. Keywords: equation of mixed type with characteristic degeneration, Keldysh problem, uniqueness, existence, series, estimate, uniform convergence.
