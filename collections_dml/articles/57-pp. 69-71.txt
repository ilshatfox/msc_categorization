ON THE INCLUSION OF GENERALISED SOBOLEV SPACES M Pα (X ) INTO CONTINUOUS FUNCTIONS IN THE CRITICAL CASE  S.A. Bondarev  Let (X ,d ,µ) be a space of homogeneous type with doubling dimension γ. Inclusion of generalized Sobolev spaces M pα (X ) (p is a summability parameter, α is a smoothness parameter) into the space of continuous functions in the critical case γ=αp is discussed. It is shown, that, under certain conditions on the space (X ,d ,µ), for p ≤ 1, α > 0, any function from M pα (X ) locally has an uniformly continuous representative.
