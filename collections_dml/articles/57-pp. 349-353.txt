Kolesnikov  Fujimori S. and Weber M. gave a method for constructing embedded triply periodic minimal surfaces in the Euclidean space, based on the Schwarz-Christoffel formula for periodic polygons of strip type. This paper considers a construction method for periodic minimal surface based on holomorphic map of the half-plane onto periodic polygon of half-plane type. We investigate two related but different problems. The first is to find the conditions on the Riesz measures υu and µM of functions u and M respectively under which there exists a subharmonic function h ̸≡ −∞ onD such that u+h < M . The second is the same question, but for a harmonic function h on D.
