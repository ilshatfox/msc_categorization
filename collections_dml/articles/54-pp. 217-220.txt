Kuznetsova  We consider C∗-algebras generated by mappings of special kind. A given mapping on a countable set induces a family of partial isometries which generates the algebra. We give conditions for ensuring that the family multiplicatively generates an inverse semigroup and study the corresponding C∗-algebras. Keywords: C∗-algebra, inverse semigroup, invariant subspace, weighted shift operator, Toeplitz algebra.
