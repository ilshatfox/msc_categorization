Katz  In this paper we study the jump problem and some certain solutions of Beltrami equation ∂φ= µ∂φ. The jump problem and Beltrami equation both have a lot of applications in physics and mechanics. They are already studied in previous papers for analytic (i.e. 0-analytic) functions. Now we shall study same problems on β-analytic functions. We shall expand our technic onto those functions and obtain new results in this field. Keywords: fractal, Marcinkiewicz exponents, metric characteristics, non-rectifiable curves.
