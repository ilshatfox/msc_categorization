Kechko  The convergence rate of type II Hermite – Padé approximants for system {eλ j z }3 j=1, where {λ j }  3 j=1 are  different nonzero complex numbers, is found. The theorems, proved in the paper, complement the results obtained earlier by O. Perron, D. Braess, A. I. Aptekarev, A. P. Starovoitov, and other authors. Keywords: simultaneous Padé approximations, type II Hermite – Padé approximations, asymptotic equalities.
