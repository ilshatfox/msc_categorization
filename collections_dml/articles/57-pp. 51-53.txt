Bednova  This article presents the solution method of the temperature problem of the sudden heating of the nonuniform in depth half-space surface. We use integral formulas that allow us to obtain the solution of the original problem for an equation with variable coefficients depending on coordinates and time, by solving the same problem for an equation with constant coefficients. Keywords: heat conductivity, partial differential equations with variable coefficients, integral formulas.
