Lukomskii, M.D. Myshko  We define binary basic splines of the second order, indicate an algorithm for constructing an interpolation polynomial of the second degree, and give an estimated deviation of the interpolation polynomial. We also prove that the constructed basic spline is a solution of some refinement equation. Keywords: binary basic spline, interpolation polynomial, refinement equation.
